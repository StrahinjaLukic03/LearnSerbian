Why Serbian?

It can help you reach 20+ million speakers worldwide due to its history and mutual inteligibility with neighbouring languages.

If you ever thought about starting your own company, Serbia offers plenty of business opportunities at a fraction of a cost.
It makes you think differently. Serbian is a complex language and offers greater freedom of expression and it'll boost your brainpower too!
It can help you learn other Slavic languages due to many similarities Slavic languages have across the board.
It can help you appreciate the culture more. Knowing Serbian you'll be able to immerse yourself in Serbias extensive history and the roles it had throughout the years. It will help you get by. Even learning a few simple phrases will help you navigate across Serbia as not many people speak English in markets or stores. Serbians are hospitable people and knowing a few phrases will make you stand out that much more i the eyes of locals.
